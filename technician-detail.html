Electrical Maintenance technician

Amman, Jordan Full Time JOD 20 - JOD 50

technician description

An Electrical Maintenance Technician is responsible for ensuring the safe and efficient operation of electrical systems and equipment within a facility or industrial environment. This role involves diagnosing electrical issues, performing preventative maintenance, and repairing or replacing electrical components as necessary. Technicians typically work with machinery, control systems, power distribution systems, lighting, and wiring in buildings or manufacturing plants.
